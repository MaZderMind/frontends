other: Barbnerdy

DJ Set:

BarbNerdy is not only nerdy in finding great music all over the places, she is also writing wonderful stories with them. . In Berlin she is doing since 2009 the very open minded and bass music focussed Support Your Local Ghetto nights at the lovely Panke with a lot of local but also international guests.
She started DJ'ing in 1990, worked with people from the first techno generation like DJ Hell, Blake Baxter or Abe Duque but already in 1996 she got bored of the 4/4 and jumped out of this strict costume and enjoyed even more new music before this hits the mainstream.
Music and bringing music to the people is her biggest passion. Give her a good sound system and she will guide you through a unforgettable journey of music.
