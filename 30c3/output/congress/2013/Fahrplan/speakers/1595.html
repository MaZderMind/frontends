Speaker: Markus Beckedahl

Markus Beckedahl blogs on netzpolitik.org and is a chair-person of Digitale Gesellschaft e. V. (@digiges). He is founder of the re:publica-conferences.

Markus Beckedahl has been blogging on netzpolitik.org about politics and the digital society since 2002. Markus is an active partner and senior consultant at newthinking GmbH and also co-founder and host of Germany's largest blogger and social media conference re:publica.
