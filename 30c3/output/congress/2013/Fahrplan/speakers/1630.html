Speaker: Constanze Kurz

Constanze is the spokeswoman of the German Chaos Computer Club.

Constanze holds a doctoral degree in computer science and works at University of Applied Sciences in Berlin. She is an expert on surveillance techniques and co-authored technical analyses for the German Constitutional Court on controversial cases like data retention, anti-terror database, and voting computers.
