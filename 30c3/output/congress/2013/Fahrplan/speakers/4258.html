Speaker: Karl Heinz Jeron

I was born in 1962 in Memmingen Germany. I live and work in Berlin. From 2000 to 2006 I have been lecturer for Multimedia Art at the University of Arts Berlin. My works have been exhibited at ZKM Karlsruhe, Ars Electronica Linz, Documenta X, ICA London, Walker Art Museum Minneapolis, Berlinischen Galerie Berlin and the Museum of Modern Art San Francisco.

I rather create experiences than objects
